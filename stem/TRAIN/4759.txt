The cabinet approved finance ministry plan raise amount outstanding government bond percentage total budget financial year 1987/88 starting July 1 , meet growing demand fund major construction project , cabinet official said .
The increase would allow outstanding bond total 40 pct 1987/88 budget , 25 pct current year .
He said increase would allow government issue bond worth 105 billion Taiwan dlrs next financial year , 49.6 billion current year .
The proposal go parliament final approval .
A finance ministry official told Reuters increase bond issue necessary finance 14 major project , including highway , power plant , underground railway receiving terminal liquefied natural gas .
Spending major project expected total 120 billion Taiwan dlrs 1987/88 , compared 89 billion current year , said .
