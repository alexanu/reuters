The Asian Development Bank ( ADB ) said approved two loan totalling 75.7 mln dlrs agricultural project Bangladesh Nepal .
Both loan repayable 40 year , including 10-year grace , carry service charge one pct year .
The bank approved 51.7 mln dlrs agricultural input program Bangladesh aimed sustaining foodgrain production .
The loan cover foreign exchange component project 's total cost 65 mln dlrs .
The ADB also approved 24 mln dlrs agricultural credit project Nepal increase foodgrain dairy produce output .
