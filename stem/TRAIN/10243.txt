The U.K .
Exported 612,000 tonne wheat 498,800 tonne barley February , Home Grown Cereals Authority ( HGCA ) said .

The new figure bring cumulative wheat export period July 1-March 13 3.66 mln tonne barley 3.50 mln , compared 1.47 2.09 mln tonne respectively last season .
