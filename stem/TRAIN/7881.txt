Kuwaiti oil minister Sheikh Ali al-Khalifa al-Sabah said OPEC producing well oil output ceiling would help price move higher , according Kuwaiti newspaper interview .
The al-Rai al-Aam newspaper quoted saying OPEC pumping 2.4 mln barrel per day ( bpd ) le 15.8 mln bpd ceiling , consumer drawing petroleum stock rate 4.5 mln bpd .
As long OPEC maintains output curb , demand oil rise April May , Sheikh Ali said , adding Kuwait strongly committed OPEC quota .
