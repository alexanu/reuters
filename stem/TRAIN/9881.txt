The Philippines World Bank signed 300 mln dlr loan 10 mln dlr worth technical assistance help Manila economic recovery effort , bank official said .
The loan , disbursed three equal tranche , repayable 20 year , including five-year grace period , variable interest rate , currently 7.92 pct .
The loan signed Finance Minister Jaime Ongpin A. Karasmasoglu , World Bank vice-president East Asia Pacific .
Ongpin said first tranche 100 mln dlrs expected within month .
