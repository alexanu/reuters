China 's trade deficit totalled 700 mln dlrs first two month year , according figure released State Statistics Bureau .
The New China News Agency quoted Bureau saying foreign trade totalled 9.3 billion dlrs period , export worth 4.3 billion dlrs .
The bureau said total trade volume 2.5 pct 1986 period , export 18.1 pct , gave comparative figure .
China 's 1986 trade deficit totalled 12 billion dlrs , official figure show .
