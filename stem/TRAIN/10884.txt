Groupement pour le financement de Ouvrages de Batiment , Travaux Publics et activites annex ( GOBTP ) plan launch two variable-rate domestic bond totalling 700 mln franc , lead manager Credit Commercial de France ( CCF ) Caisse Centrale de Banques Populaires ( CCBP ) said .
The first 500 mln franc 12-year bond , led CCF , issued 99 pct interest based annualised money market rate ( TAM ) payable April 21 every year first coupon paid next year .
The second 200 mln franc 12-year bond , led CCBP , issued 99 pct interest also based TAM coupon payment date .
Both bond principle redeemable par April 21 , 1999 .
