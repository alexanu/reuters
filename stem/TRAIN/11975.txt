Hongkong Shanghai Banking Corp Standard Chartered Bank raised prime rate one-half point 6.5 pct , effective Tuesday , Association Banks said .
The association said statement deposit rate also increased 1/4 1/2 percentage point .
The bank last announced adjustment February 28 raised prime rate one point six pct .
