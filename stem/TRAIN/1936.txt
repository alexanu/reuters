The Italian treasury said would offer 3,500 billion lira short-term treasury bill ( BOTs ) rate slightly lower preceding offer mid-February .
It said would offer 1,500 billion lira six-month paper priced 95.35 pct net annualised compound yield 9.30 pct .
Net yield preceding issue 9.46 pct .
The treasury said would also offer 2,000 billion lira 12-month bill base price 91.15 pct net annual yield 9.05 pct , 9.22 pct mid-February .
The bill replace maturing paper worth 3,196 billion lira subscription offer close March 10 .
