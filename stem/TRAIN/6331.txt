U.S. oil demand measured product supplied rose 2.3 pct four week ended March 13 16.49 mln barrel per day ( bpd ) 16.11 mln period year ago , Energy Information Administration ( EIA ) said .
In weekly petroleum status report , Energy Department agency said distillate demand 2.9 pct period 3.43 mln bpd 3.33 mln year earlier .
Gasoline demand averaged 6.93 mln bpd , 4.0 pct 6.67 mln last year , residual fuel demand 1.31 mln bpd , 7.9 pct 1.42 mln , EIA said .
Domestic crude oil production estimated 8.36 mln bpd , 8.1 pct 9.10 mln year ago , gross daily crude import ( excluding SPR ) averaged 3.70 mln bpd , 24.8 pct 2.96 mln , EIA said .
Refinery crude run four week 11.92 mln bpd , 1.0 pct 11.80 mln year earlier , said .
