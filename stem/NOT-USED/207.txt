The Thai Cabinet approved seven pct increase national budget fiscal 1987/88 starting next October , compared 6.2 pct increase current year ending September , government spokesman said .
He told reporter budget , presented Parliament late next month early June , call spending 243.5 billion baht 227.5 billion year .
The new budget intended make Thailand 's Gross Domestic Product grow least five pct year without compromising conservative fiscal monetary policy , spokesman said .
`` The budget line policy stimulate economy achieve stable growth continue maintaining current cautious fiscal policy , '' said .
The spokesman said budget provides 9.6 pct increase fresh government investment expenditure 39.8 billion baht , 36.3 billion year .
Government revenue projected grow 7.5 pct 199.5 billion baht 185.5 billion targetted 1986/87 .
He said budget contains 44 billion baht deficit , 3.4 pct GDP , compared 42 billion 3.5 pct planned year .
The deficit required Thai law financed domestic borrowing .
The finalised budget bill earmark 59.8 billion baht , 24.6 pct total expenditure , servicing government's domestic foreign debt , 56 billion similar 24.6 pct year .
