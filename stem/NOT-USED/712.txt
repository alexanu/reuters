Italy 's wholesale price index rose 0.5 pct month-on-month April , increasing 0.1 pct March , national statistic institute Istat said .
The index , base 1980 equal 100 , registered 174.2 April compared 173.3 March .
The April figure represents increase 1.6 pct compared corresponding month 1986 , year-on-year rise March year 0.6 pct .
