Japan 's broadly defined money supply average M-2 plus certificate deposit ( CD ) jumped preliminary 10.2 pct May year earlier , Bank Japan said .

The money supply increased 9.8 pct April .
The seasonally adjusted average May rose 1.0 pct previous month .
