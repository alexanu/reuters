Parliament passed law allowing state-owned industrial company sell 49 pct share staff private investor , official BSS news agency said .
Under new law , passed yesterday , 51 pct share affected company held government , 34 pct public 15 pct staff .
The law also allows worker nominate member board director buy least 12 pct company 's share .
Bangladesh returned 500 industrial company private ownership since March 1982 .
