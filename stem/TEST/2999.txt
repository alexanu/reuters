Inter-Regional Financial Group Inc said IFG Leasing Co , discontinued subsidiary , redeem , effective August One , 4.0 mln dlr outstanding balance 11-1/2 pct subordinated note due Aug 1 , 1988 .
It said also redeem , effective September One , 3.5 mln dlr outstanding balance 13 pct junior subordinated note due Sept 1 , 1990 .
The company said expects redeem final 4.2 mln dlrs subordinated debt end September .
Separately , Inter-Regional Financial said completed agreement group bank new two-year 30 mln dlr revolving credit facility .
It said agreement replaces current 18 mln dlr revolving credit agreement .
