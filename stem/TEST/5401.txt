Kuwait 's oil export affected seven-year Iran-Iraq war , Kuwait Oil Tanker Company ( KOTC ) Chairman Managing Director Abdul Fattah al-Bader told Kuwaiti newspaper .
`` Kuwait exploited available opportunity continue exporting oil without reduction , '' Al-Anbaa newspaper quoted saying .
He said KOTC made profit two mln dinar ( seven mln dlrs ) fiscal year ending last June , predicted lower profit year due higher cost chartering operating vessel .
