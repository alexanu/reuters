Brazil bought 75,000 tonne French wheat tender , Brazilian Wheat Board spokesman said .
He said Board accepted offer 25,000 tonne wheat grain firm J. Souffle 80.49 dlrs per tonne Fob August shipment .
For September shipment , Board bought 25,000 tonne Graniere 79.32 dlrs per tonne Fob , October shipment accepted 25,000 tonne Andre Companie 79.47 dlrs per tonne Fob .
The next tender , Aug/Sept/Oct shipment , set June 24 , spokesman said .
