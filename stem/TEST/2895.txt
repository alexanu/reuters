The Belgian National Bank said cut one , two , three month treasury certificate rate , 0.10 point , effective immediately .
The cut take one month rate 6.75 pct , two month rate 6.80 pct key three month rate 6.85 pct , central bank said telex .
A National Bank spokesman said modest cut made decline domestic money market rate yesterday , expected followed cut 7.60 pct discount rate .
The short-term treasury certificate rate , especially three-month rate , National Bank 's main monetary policy instrument last two year .
The discount rate market 's penalty rate rather guiding rate since 1985 , advance currently little used liquidity ample , bank economist said .
The bank last cut one- three-month certificate rate June 5 , also 0.10 point .
