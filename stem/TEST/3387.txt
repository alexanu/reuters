Dutch seasonally adjusted industrial production fell 6.4 pct April compared previous month , 5.5 pct period last year , figure official Statistics Bureau CBS show .
The April index , base 1980 , stood 103 .
In March , industrial production rose 2.8 pct February .
The unadjusted April index figure fell 14 pct March 104 , compared 10 pct rise March February .
