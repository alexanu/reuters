Italy 's wholesale price index rose 0.2 pct month-on-month February 1987 increasing 1.1 pct January , national statistic institute ISTAT said .
The index , base 1980 equal 100 , registered 173.1 February compared 172.8 January .
The February figure represents decline 0.2 pct compared February 1986 year-on-year decline January 1987 1.7 pct .
