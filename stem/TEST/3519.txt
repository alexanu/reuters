Banque Arabe et Internationale d'Investissement ( BAII ) issue one billion franc non-callable pre-placed bond three tranche July 8 , lead manager Banque Nationale de Paris ( BNP ) said .
Banque Indosuez manage 200 mln franc `` A '' tranche , issued par life nine year , paying interest based annualised money market rate ( TAM ) plus 0.10 pct .
The 250 mln-franc `` B '' tranche , managed Banque d'Arbitrage et de Credit ( BAC ) , issued par life 10 year , paying interest TAM plus 0.15 pct .
The 550 mln-franc `` C '' tranche , managed BNP Banque Demachy et Associes , issued par life 11 year , paying interest TAM plus 0.20 pct .
