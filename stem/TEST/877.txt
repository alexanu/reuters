Spain 's foreign reserve rose 905 mln dlrs February total 17.05 billion dlrs , compared 14.11 billion dlrs February 1986 , Bank Spain figure show .
Under new accounting system introduced month , Spain 's foreign reserve exclude foreign currency held financial institution .
Under previous system , Spain 's foreign reserve would risen 391 mln dlrs February , taking account fall 514 mln dlrs financial institution ' foreign currency position .
In January year , Central Bank 's foreign reserve rose 143 mln dlrs , financial institution ' foreign currency position fell 118 mln dlrs .
