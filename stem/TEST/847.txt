Colombian export coffee rose 55 pct January compared period last year , figure government statistic institute show .
Non-coffee export amounted 180.8 mln dlrs fob compared 147.5 mln dlrs coffee , drop 42 pct last year .
The trade balance registered 35 mln dlr surplus , compared 56 mln dlr surplus January 1986 .
The national planning department forecast 1987 coffee , Colombia 's traditional major export , account one third total export , 1.5 billion dlrs .
